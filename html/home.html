Wet Bloom

"Wet Bloom" portrays the beauty of flowers in the rain. With vibrant colors and glistening petals, the artwork captures the freshness and vitality of nature after a downpour. It invites viewers to appreciate the beauty and resilience of life even in the midst of adversity.
